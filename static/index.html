Lay The Draw — Mismatched Games Only

We bet that a clear favourite will score a goal, making a draw impossible. When they do, draw odds spike and we cash out for a guaranteed profit. We only play games where draw odds are ≥ 4.5 — this cuts the draw rate from 21% down to just 6%.

How it works — step by step

1

Find a mismatched game

We check pre-match draw odds on Smarkets. If draw odds are ≥ 4.5 (e.g. Brazil vs Haiti, Germany vs Curaçao), one team is a clear favourite. We proceed.

2

Lay the draw pre-match

We lay the draw — betting it WON'T end level. Liability = stake × (odds − 1). At £200 stake and 5.0 odds, liability = £800.

3

Goal scored after 45 min → cash out for profit

When the favourite scores, draw odds spike from 5.0 to ~12.0. We back the draw at the new high odds. This locks in guaranteed profit regardless of final result.

4

Still 0-0 at 70 min → limit the loss

If no goal by 70 min, draw odds shorten. We back the draw at lower odds to cap our loss. Without this, a 0-0 draw costs the full liability.

Example: Brazil vs Haiti (draw odds 7.0, stake £200)

Pre-match: Lay draw @ 7.0Stake £200, liability £1,200
Brazil scores at 55 min → draw odds spike to 15.0—
Back draw @ 15.0, stake £93—
If Brazil wins (likely)+£107 profit
If it somehow draws+£105 profit
Result: ~£106 locked profit guaranteed✓

⚠ Risks

  • 0-0 draw = full liability lost (~£800 at £200 stake). Use the 70-min exit to limit this.
  • 3–4 draws in a row can wipe many wins. Need £3,000+ bankroll.
  • In-play odds move in seconds — bot must execute immediately after a goal.
  • This is gambling. Past results do not guarantee future profits.
